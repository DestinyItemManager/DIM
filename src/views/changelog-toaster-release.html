v3.16 - Make Room for Postmaster

We have a walk through of the Compare Items feature on YouTube. There are several other walk throughs in the collection.

What's new in this release...

  • A new "Make room for Postmaster items" auto layout will clear out enough space on your character to pick up all the stuff you've accumulated at the Postmaster.
  • Vendor items now explain what you need to do to get them.
  • Resolved issues with Loadouts not transfering unequipable items.
  • Compare tool will highlight item in your inventory when clicking on its name.
  • Added stat search, for example: "stat:rof:>= 22".
