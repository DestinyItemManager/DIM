Supporting DIM

DIM is a product that is free to download and use. The source code for DIM is open sourced, and free to anyone to enhance. You will never see an ad in DIM. That is our committment.

We're all huge fans of Destiny. DIM came to life in those hours of gameplay because we felt that pain of transferring gear between characters and forgetting that one item, or trips down to the tower to unload your ROC strikes. DIM is the tool that will help you get more gametime.

How can I contribute?

Donate to DIM
We use PayPal to accept donations. If DIM has made a difference in your gametime, support the team working on the project.
Share the DIM with your friends!
Once you've experienced how easy it is to equip a loadout, you'll be hooked. Share the fun with your friends!
Do you have an idea for DIM?
Visit us on /r/destinyitemmanager and let us know about your idea. You'll hear from us as well as the community around DIM which is a great way to iterate and improve an idea.
I'm a developer/designer/enthusist and I'm looking to contribute to the source.
Visit us on GitHub and send one of the contributors a message. We're more than happy to accept pull requests. Join us as a contributor and join our meetings where we talk about the next version, feature updates, or crushing bugs.
