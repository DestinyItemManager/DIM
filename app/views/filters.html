Filters

Click filters below to apply, or by typing in the search box.

Filters are combined using 'and' logic, for example "is:arc light:>300" will return all arc items with over 300 light.

Filter Description
item name Shows items where their name has a partial match to the filter text.
is:arc is:solar is:void is:kinetic Shows items based on their damage type.
light:value light:>=value light:>value light:<value light:<=value Shows items based on their light level (Attack for Weapons; Defense for Armor).
is:class is:primary is:special is:heavy Shows items based on their weapon classes.
is:helmet is:gauntlets is:chest is:leg is:classitem Shows armors based on their category.
is:common is:uncommon is:rare is:legendary is:exotic Shows items based on their rarity tier.
is:ascended is:unascended Shows items that have an ascend node and if they have been ascended or not.
is:reforgeable is:reforge is:rerollable is:reroll Shows items that can be reforged at the Gunsmith.
is:complete is:incomplete is:xpincomplete, is:needsxp is:xpcomplete is:upgraded Shows items at various states of leveling:
  • Complete shows items that are totally complete - every upgrade unlocked.
  • Incomplete shows items that are not complete - there's still at least one upgrade to unlock.
  • xpincomplete/needsxp shows items that can still have XP put into them.
  • xpcomplete shows items that cannot have XP put into them (whether or not their upgrades have been unlocked).
  • Upgraded shows items that have enough XP to unlock all their nodes, but not all the nodes have been unlocked.
is:titan is:hunter is:warlock Shows items based on their class affinty.
is:dupe is:duplicate Shows duplicate items
is:locked is:unlocked Shows items based on their locked status.
is:stackable Shows items that can stack (ammo synths, strange coin, etc)
is:pulserifle is:scoutrifle is:handcannon is:autorifle is:sniperrifle is:shotgun is:fusionrifle is:sidearm is:rocketlauncher is:machinegun is:sword is:primaryweaponengram is:specialweaponengram is:heavyweaponengram Shows weapons based on their weapon type.
is:engram Shows engrams.
is:year1 is:year2 Shows items from which year of Destiny they appeared in.
