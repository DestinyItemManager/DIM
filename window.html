FAQ
  • Why is this app so slow? It seems like it's only moving an item at a time.
    Unfortunately, the bungie API limits the number of calls made to their service to 1 call per second. Faster than flying to tower though :)
  • How can I contact you? Most bug/suggestion/comments are best directed this way.
    I can best be contacted through my reddit post here.
  • I lost my item using your tool!
    Try reloading the page and then using the search function. I'm sure it's still there.
  • It's hard to see everything on the screen to do drag and drop effectively!
    Page is best viewed at 1648px width if your monitor supports it. Otherwise, click an item to open a move menu.
  • Thank you! Can I contribute in any way?
    Share the tool and message me with your ideas, I would love to hear them!
